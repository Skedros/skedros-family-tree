Help us fill in the tree.

This site is built from family records, the 1974 Helen Skedros Rizos interview, and conversations with relatives. If you have stories, photographs, exact dates, or corrections that would help — please send them here. Everything goes straight to Athan to be reviewed and added to the site.

You don't need to fill in every field. The most useful things are stories in your own words, photos with names on the back, and clear corrections of anything we've gotten wrong.
